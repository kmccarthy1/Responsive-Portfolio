About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Kevin and Richmond, VA is my hometown!
I'm excited to learn the ins and outs of being a full stack devloper and dream of using
my skills to help others in the future.

I spent the first 18 years of my life in Richmond before going to college in Fredericksburg, VA
at the University of Mary Washington. While there, I competed for their collegiate Cross
Country and Track & Field teams. Although I no longer compete for the university, my love
for running has not ended. I'm currently training to race the Richmond Marathon in November; a race I see as equally thrilling and terrifying.

My interest for coding & development began in college, but never really went anywhere as I pursued a career as a GIS Analyst using the skills I learned in the GIS (Geographic Information Systems) field. Although, I'm still interested in GIS, I learned it wasn't something I could do forever with a small job market and automation on the horizon. I'm excited to hone my skills in the coming months and be able to show off cool projects here on this site!
